Medical Services

Diagnostic Services

Our hospital is equipped with cutting-edge diagnostic tools and technologies to accurately assess your health condition and aid in the formulation of appropriate treatment plans. Our diagnostic services include:

  • Imaging Services: We utilize advanced imaging techniques such as X-rays, CT scans, MRI scans, ultrasound, and mammography to obtain detailed images of your body, aiding in the diagnosis and monitoring of various conditions.
  • Laboratory Services: Our state-of-the-art laboratories perform a wide range of tests, including blood tests, urine analysis, genetic testing, pathology evaluations, and more, facilitating accurate and timely diagnoses.

Surgeries

Our skilled surgical team is experienced in performing a variety of surgical procedures to address various medical conditions. We offer:

  • General Surgeries: Including appendectomy, gallbladder removal, hernia repair, and more.
  • Orthopedic Surgeries: Specializing in joint replacements, spine surgeries, fracture repairs, and more.
  • Cardiothoracic Surgeries: Expertise in heart bypass surgery, valve repair/replacement, and other cardiac procedures.

Emergency Care

Our hospital's emergency department is available 24/7 to provide immediate medical attention to patients with urgent healthcare needs. Our emergency care services include:

  • Trauma Care: Our trauma team is equipped to handle severe injuries and accidents.
  • Cardiac Emergencies: We provide rapid response and treatment for heart attacks and other cardiac emergencies.
  • Stroke Care: Our stroke center offers specialized care for stroke patients, including timely interventions.
